Kids and codestories

We are a team dedicated to teach children coding from the ground up so thath they grow up to become masters of the game. We believe that starting form the foundation and building on through the years makes for a firmer grasp in an industry that is fast growing and domination the world. Coding can be fun, interesting and lucrative. We believe that giving the children an early opportunity only prepares them for success in future.

Our focus

We envision a future where children are made job for the future with the right technical and soft skills.

Children's environment

Our vision is to create a fun and enlightening coding environment to help children reach their full pontential. We take care to make teaching plans that are child friendly with alot of fun activities to keep the child interested but still challenged.​

Supervisions

We believe that a child's growth and success is tied to many factors. We love to involve parents in the supervisions of their child's activities so that they can appreciate the development of their children.

-->

Soft skills

We aim at developing an all-rounded child. We have learnt that in this ever evolving world, soft skills in many cases can be more appreciated than the actual technical skills. We have come up with a character development plan where we mainly lean into bring out the child's leadership and communication skills. We believe a combination of the two sets of skills builds an all-rounded individual.
